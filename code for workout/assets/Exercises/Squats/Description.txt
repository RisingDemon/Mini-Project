	1.Squats

	- Keep your feet slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
	- Keep your chest and chin up push your hips back and bend your knees.
	- Ensuring your knees dont bow inward or outward, drop down until your thighs are parallel to the ground.
	- Pause for one second, then extend your legs and return to the starting position.
