	2.Side to Side Jump Squats

	- Place your feet shoulder-width apart. Bend at the
	  knees while driving your hips back.
	- Keep your chest up and your core tight.
	- Remain in a squat stance as you forcefully push off
	  the ground, jumping to the right side.
	- Check your form then repeat, jumping to the other side.
	- Keep alternating between sides.
