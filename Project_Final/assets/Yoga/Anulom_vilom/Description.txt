	4.Anulom Vilom

	- Sit any meditative pose and using your right hand, fold your middle and index fingers.
	- Close right nostril with your thumb and inhale through left nostril, deeply.
	- Release your thumb and close left nostril with your ring finger.
	- Exhale slowly through right nostril.
	- Now do it in reverse, inhaling through right nostril and exhaling through the left.
