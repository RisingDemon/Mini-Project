	6.Bicycles
	
	- Lie on your back and bring your legs up Bend your
	  elbows, and put your hands behind your head.
	- Crunch up and bring your right elbow to your left
	  knee, straightening your right leg.
	- Release the crunch slightly, and do the same with
	  your left knee.
